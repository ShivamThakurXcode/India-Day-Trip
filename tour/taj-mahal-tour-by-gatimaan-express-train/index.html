Tour Overview

Superfast Train from Delhi With Taj Mahal Tour by Gatimaan Express, you will travel in India’s superfast train from Delhi. The superfast train, Gatimaan Express takes only one and a half hours from Delhi to reach Agra, where the timeless beauty of the Taj Mahal awaits you. Enjoy the exquisite architecture and learn about its captivating history with a knowledgeable guide. Explore Agra Fort and enjoy a delicious lunch while experiencing the rich history of the city. Traveling by train is the safest and fastest way to see the magnificent Taj Mahal and the city of Agra up close.

Tour Itinerary

Delhi – Agra (By Gatimaan Express Train @ 08.10 AM)

You will be picked up in the morning from your hotel in Delhi and then transfer to Hazrat Nizamuddin railway station to board Gatimaan Express train to Agra. It is India’s first semi-high speed train. Breakfast will be served in Train.

Arrival Agra(9.50 AM)

Arrive Agra and meet with our representative or private guide and he will assist you to visit mesmerizingTaj Mahal– “One of the seven wonders of the world”. The beauty of this magnificent monument is such that it is beyond the scope of words. See the white marble work, Calligraphy, lattice work, fountains, pools, mosque etc. inside the Taj Mahal.

Afternoon you will enjoy lunch at local restaurant to enjoy the local delicacies and cuisines.

After lunch you will visitAgra Fortwhich was begun by Emperor Akbar between 1565 and 1573. It is situated on the west bank of the Yamuna River, about 2km upstream from the Taj Mahal. Get the information about Courtyard, Khas Mahal, Hall of Private Audience, Nagina Masjid, Mirror Palace and the ladies bazaar inside the fort.

If time permits, you will visitItimad-Ud-Daulah Tomb, also known as baby Taj. This is precisely because is the very first tomb in India that is entirely made out of Marble.

Agra – Delhi (By Gatimaan Express Train @05.50 PM)

In the evening you will be transferred to Agra Railway Station to board the train to return Delhi. Arrive Delhi and then transfer to your hotel.
